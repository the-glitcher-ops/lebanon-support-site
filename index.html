⚠️ Important Legal Guidelines

  • Avoid Unregistered Crowdfunding: Personal links on platforms like GoFundMe can be frozen by banks due to international compliance issues.
  • Do Not Wire Directly to Strangers: Direct Western Union or bank transfers to unknown individuals carry high risks of fraud or AML (Anti-Money Laundering) flagging.
  • Tax Deductions: Check if the organization has a sister 501(c)(3) (if in the US) or equivalent in your home country for tax-deductible receipts.
